About Us

Welcome to Thrifted Treasures — your ultimate destination for unique finds and sustainable fashion! We're not just a thrift store; we’re a community driven by creativity, affordability, and a love for second chances.

Our Story

What started as weekend thrift adventures turned into a passion project — and eventually, Thrifted Treasures was born. We are here to rescue beautiful clothing and give it a new life with someone who will cherish it.

Our Mission

Campus Thrift was born from a simple idea: students have great items they no longer need, and other students are looking for affordable alternatives to buying new. We created a platform that makes it easy to buy and sell within your campus community.

Our mission is to reduce waste, save students money, and create a circular economy on college campuses across the country.
